Patent Analysis

At Nilan Ventures, our Patent Analysis services are designed to help inventors, startups, and organizations make informed, strategic decisions around their intellectual property.

What We Offer:

  • Patentability Assessments: Evaluate whether your innovation meets the criteria for a patent.
  • Prior Art Searches: Identify existing patents, publications, and technologies that may impact originality.
  • Freedom-to-Operate (FTO) Analysis: Determine if your technology can be commercialized without infringing on existing IP.
  • Patent Landscape Reports: Understand market trends, R&D opportunities, and competitors.
  • Claim Charting & Infringement Analysis: Critical insights for licensing or litigation readiness.

Why It Matters: Our detailed analysis reduces risk, uncovers opportunities, and enables confident innovation-driven growth.

Copyright Services
